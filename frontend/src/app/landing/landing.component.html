Welcome

LiveQuiz is a minimalistic tool to offer live quizzes to your students. It offers the following functionalities:

  • Managing quizzes by courses
  • Adding / modifying / removing quizzes to / from courses
  • Activating a quiz for your students
  • Showing progress of the students in the quiz
  • Correcting the quiz, showing worst answers first

And of course it should one day join the Fledger network, so that all of the storage is decentralized...
